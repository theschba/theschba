The SCHBA meets the second Monday of each month from March through November at 7:30 PM in the meeting room of Lakeview Apts, Bath, NY. The exception is the August meeting, which takes place in the Agriculture Building at the Steuben County Fairgrounds as we set up our annual display of honey, honey products, and an observation hive.
Currently our meeting place is closed due to covid-19.
Some years we host a field day. On a Saturday in July we open up hives and learn together, and we have a dinner for members and friends in November.

Newcomers are always welcome at our meetings.
