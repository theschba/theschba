NYS Bee Inspector
We have a new bee inspector in our area. The message below is from him. He inspected our apiary for disease and bee health; we found him friendly and amiable.

My name is Matthew Eaton, New York State Apiary Inspector for the western regions of New York. The state has opened up general inspections due to loosening restrictions surrounding COVID-19. I would love the opportunity to inspect as many apiaries as possible. You are not required to be registered for an inspection. So if your are open or welcoming an inspection over the course of the next two weeks, I should be focusing on your county. You can contact me at +13154151697.  I look forward to hearing from you.
